My Very Loving and Caring Mama

Our boss, the first woman I loved, our spiritual guide and my number one supporter, mama Myra.

Dear Mama,

Merry Christmas, Ma. I would like to tell you how much grateful our family with you. you are truly the wind beneath our wings. You are our number supporter in just everything we are in, may it be academics, social life, and as well as during our down times. You are the first woman I loved and will always love. Ma, you always teach me the value of faith and praying. You are one of my greatest influence next to papa, asy you teach me how to balance intelligence with emotional aspects of life and spirituality. You are the greatest care taker, the greatest cook, and the greatest alarm clock. You are the greatest mother a family could ever had. You have taught us that God is with us, even in the darkest time, and family is something we should always watch each other's back no matter what happens. To more Christmas we will celebrate together and I hope, every Christmas, we can celebrate it with Papa. Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year, Mama Myra. I love you to the outside of the milky way galaxy and back.

Love your son,

Alexis
