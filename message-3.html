My Ever-So-Funny Brother

Our comedian, player, and mr. congeniality of the family, Aldrei.

Dear Aldrei,

Merry Christmas, Dudong. I want to thank you for always trying to not fight me back whenever I told you to do something or when we are quarreling over an idea. You are a best friend and the best brother one could have. You're jokes are always on time whenever we need something to cheer us up. You are a very nice, kind, but sometimes careless boy. Thank you for always being so thoughtful of me, I appreciate your gifts to me, even though I am not as vocal of what I felt to you. My brother, behave and continue to be a very caring and a street smart person. You are one of a kind. Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year, Aldrei. I love you to the outside of the milky way galaxy and back.

Yours truly,

Kuya Alexis
